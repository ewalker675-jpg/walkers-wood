Do you offer premium firewood delivery to Worcester?

Yes, Walkers Wood provides free firewood and seasoned log delivery to homes, traditional country pubs, and holiday lets within a 10-mile radius of our base, including Worcester city centre, Martley, and Clifton upon Teme. We supply top-quality hardwood split and ready for open fires and wood burners.

Why Choose Walkers Wood in Worcester?

We supply only the finest quality seasoned hardwood logs, sourced responsibly from our own land and local estates across Worcestershire and Herefordshire. By managing our woodlands sustainably and drying our firewood locally, we ensure that every log burns hot, clean, and with minimal smoke.

Our Worcester Delivery Zone

Delivery is completely free for addresses within 10 miles of our base in Clifton upon Teme (which covers Worcester city centre, Martley, Hallow, Broadwas, and local rural areas). For addresses between 10 and 20 miles, a small delivery charge of £2 per mile is automatically calculated at checkout.

We deliver using our own vehicles and drop off right where you need it. Stacking services are also available for £30 per cubic metre to make stocking up as easy as possible.
