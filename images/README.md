## Tips
- **Hero images** should be at least 1440px wide, landscape, ~900px tall
- **Product images** should be at least 800×500px, landscape
- **Gallery images** can be any landscape photo of the farm, wood, delivery etc
- **Use JPG** for photos, **PNG** for logos/badges with transparency
- Keep file sizes reasonable (compress with TinyPNG or similar)
- The Wix site images can be downloaded and renamed to match these filenames
