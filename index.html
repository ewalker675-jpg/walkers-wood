Our Story

Meet Ed, 9th generation farmer

I'm Ed Walker, and I run Walkers Wood from our family farm in Clifton upon Teme, Worcestershire. The Walker family has farmed this land for 100 years — working with the countryside, not against it.

Our hardwood is split and dried right here on the farm. Most of the wood we source comes directly from the farm — some comes from other local farms within Worcestershire and Herefordshire, and we do not sell wood from any further afield.

Not all firewood is equal. Ours is certified.
