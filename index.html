Our Story

Meet Ed, 9th generation farmer

I'm Ed Walker, and I run Walkers Wood from our organic family farm in Clifton upon Teme, Worcestershire. My family has farmed this land since the 1700s — nine generations of working with the countryside, not against it.

Every log we sell comes from sustainably managed woodland on and around our farm. We fell, process and deliver it all ourselves — no middlemen, no mystery about where your firewood comes from.
