01 Core service

Woodland & Site Clearance

We offer full woodland and site clearance services — from first thinning and coppicing through to final fell and complete site clearance. Whether you need a single tree removing or an entire woodland managing, we have the equipment and experience to handle it safely and efficiently.

We also specialise in ash dieback management, helping landowners safely remove affected trees before they become a hazard. All usable timber is processed into firewood, so nothing goes to waste.

Wood Processing
