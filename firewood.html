What's in the mix

Our wood species

Primary

Oak

The king of firewood. Dense, slow-burning and packed with energy. Oak produces a long-lasting, steady heat that's ideal for wood burners and open fires alike. The backbone of every load we send out.

Premium

Ash

Burns beautifully even when freshly cut — but ours is split, dried for peak performance. Ash lights easily, produces excellent heat and very little smoke. A favourite among seasoned burners.

Aromatic

Cherry

A wonderful aromatic hardwood that fills the room with a sweet, fruity scent. Cherry burns with a lovely flame pattern and moderate heat — perfect for evenings by the fire or outdoor fire pits.
